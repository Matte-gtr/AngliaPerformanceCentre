Peterborough Servicing and Repairs

We offer a full variety of servicing and repair options, whether it's time for a cambelt change, you need a major or minor service, your car is not running right, or you're just looking to get a full winter car check, we are here to help.

Servicing

Servicing a car is an important part of a vehicles maintenance. The main purposes of a service are to reduce the chance and cost of repairs over a vehicles lifetime, to change a vehicles fluids and service parts in order for your car to run at maximum efficiency, to catch issues before they become more costly and serious and to ensure your car is safe on the road.

There are a multitude of parts that we can service on your car;

  • Oil change/Routine service
  • Fuel service
  • Car Batteries
  • Cam belts
  • Shocks and suspension
  • Steering
  • Exhausts
  • Brakes
  • Clutch replacement

Repairs

If you are having trouble with your vehicle, we have the tools and experience to get your car back on the road. Our team of experienced technicians have access to some of the best diagnostics tools available, so you can bring your car to us with confidence that we will get it back to you in good working order.
