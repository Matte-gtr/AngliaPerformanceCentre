Peterborough Diagnostics

At APC, we have a tested system of diagnosis-to-repair, we won't just throw parts at the issue and hope for the best. Instead we use an educated and methodical approach towards the issue in hand, we analyze the data available to us via our daignostic equipment where possible, and if this doesn't show us the issue, we draw from our years of diagnostic experience to manually find and repair the issue.

Our combined years of experience do come in handy but the raw data from varoius sensors often paints the best picture that will lead towards a succesful repair.
