Corner Weight Setup

Peterborough Corner Weight Setup

Corner weighing tells you the overall weight of your car as well as the weight present at each wheel. Uneven corner weights will cause your car to have different levels of grip on each tyre, which can make your car unpredictable during circuit driving and high speed cornering. Good weight distribution between the front and rear of the car can also allow traction to be increased on the driven wheels for improved acceleration and balance.

At APC, We have a corner weight scale system which enables us to set your car ready for any type of circuit driving. The improvements can be vast as we can shift the weight distribution to the one that works best for you and your car and driving style.

There are some parts we can install (if you haven't already) that will help get the most out of your corner weight setup, they are as follows;
  • Adjustable coilovers
  • Adjustable droplinks
