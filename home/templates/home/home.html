About us

We are a family run garage, which emerged from a passion and love of cars. We have been running the garage for the past 11 years and have over 60 years of combined mechanical knowledge under one roof. The owner, Bart, comes from a motorsport/engineering background whos passion transcends into cusomers vehicles. Read more...

Servicing

At APC, we service a vast array of cars on a daily basis. We cover all aspects of servicing from winter checks and oil change services to major services, cambelt and clutch replacements, and even full Nissan GTR and specialist vehile gearbox strip & rebuilds. There is no car too big or small. We also pride ourselves in unbiased advice and will happily answer any questions you might have. Read more...

Diagnostics and Repairs

We have a long proven track of succesful diagnostics and repairs. We use vast array of diagnostic tools ranging from Snap-on, AUtel or Launch to VW/BMW Specifics and PIWIS. We also have facilities to test and repair ECUs. We prefer to accuratley diagnose and repair problems rather than trying a range of parts before getting to the cause of the problem. Read more...

Motorsport/Track Preparation

We offer track and drift prepapation for customers vehicles, whether it be a weekend track car or a serious racing machine. We also offer corner weight setup. We also offer panel fabrication, full forged engine builds, diff rebuilds (we use and recommend WAVERTAC) and polybushing, to name a few. Read more...

Other Services

We pride ourselves a variety of other services, including custom exhaust systems, prototyping billet parts for GR6 gearboxes, we also cater for custom fabrication ie: intake systems, intercoolers, custom engine swaps etc. For a more comprehensive list of what we offer, please have a look under our Services tab or get in touch with us to discuss your requirements.
