About Us

Anglia Performance Centre, Peterborough

Based out of Peterborough, Anglia Performance Centre was started back in 2011, this was a new chapter for me (Bart) as I was a one man band, working from home at the time.

I Started out as a VW apprentice, then moved on to University, and by a stroke of luck whilst working for Suzuki, I was approached to join a drag racing team.
This brought about a whole different side of engineering for me, the level of organisation and efficiency required was higher than anything I had experienced previously and I can gladly say, I gained some very valuable knowledge from my time there.

Following on from there, I gathered my tools, experience and rolling road and moved into my new premises, The rest is history, Anglia Performance Centre was born.

Fast forward to {% now "Y" %}, one of the many things thats sets us apart others in the industry these days is our honesty policy; we won't lie to our customers and will always share our honest opinion.
We try to be as transparent as possible and are always willing to help.
We are also always looking to push the boundaries to meet customers requirements and expectations, whether that's to fix your car, diagnose a fault, set your car up for the race track or completely rebuild your car from the ground up, we are there for our customers through every step of the process.

Bart Jakubowski - Owner/Director.
